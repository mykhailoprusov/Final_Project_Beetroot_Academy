Best dinners for muscle gain

Spicy Chicken With Couscous

This super-tasty macro-balanced meal is perfect for nailing that meal prep. Bursting with flavour, it’s a simple way to liven up your routine from plain old chicken and rice.

  • 1 tbsp curry paste
  • 1 tbsp mango chutney
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 serving salt (to taste)
  • 50 ml olive oil
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 300 g couscous
  • 350 ml vegetable stock

Speedy Harissa Chicken & Tabbouleh

Liven up your chicken meal prep with this quick harissa chicken and tabbouleh – packed with protein and healthy carbohydrates.

  • 50 g harissa paste
  • 1 tsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pinch sea salt
  • 3 x chicken breast (try skin-on for extra flavour)
  • 180 g bulgur wheat or couscous (dry weight)
  • 40 g parsley (stems and leaves)
  • 20 g mint leaves
  • 6-8 x spring onions
  • 1/2 a cucumber
  • 4 x tomatoes
  • 6 tbsp Greek yoghurt
  • 1/2 a lemon (juice and zest)
  • 1 clove of garlic (minced)
  • 1 pinch of sea salt
  • 1 handful of pomegranate seeds (optional)

Low-carb Loaf Tin Lasagne

Craving lasagne, but don’t want to make a whole tray of the stuff? Try loaf tin lasagne. You’ll get 3 easy portions for 3 days’ worth of low-carb, high-protein meal prep.

  • 1 tsp. Coconut Oil
  • 1 white onion, coarsely chopped (or 1 tbsp. onion powder)
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped (or 1 tsp. garlic powder)
  • 1 tbsp. dried oregano
  • 350g turkey mince
  • 600g chopped tomatoes or tomato passata
  • 300g lasagne sheets
  • 1 courgette
  • 1 tsp. sea salt and black pepper
  • 400g cottage cheese
  • 3 egg whites
  • 100g low-fat cheese (grated)
